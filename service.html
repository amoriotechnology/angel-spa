      Manicure

      A nail spa manicure is a luxurious and therapeutic treatment designed to pamper your hands and nails. This service typically begins with a warm soak to soften the skin and nails, followed by a gentle exfoliation to remove dead skin cells. The nails are then carefully trimmed, shaped, and buffed to perfection. Cuticle care is provided to ensure healthy nail growth, and a relaxing hand massage with moisturizing lotion follows to hydrate and rejuvenate the skin.

      The manicure is completed with the application of nail polish in your choice of color or a clear gloss for a natural look. Some nail spas offer additional options, such as gel polish, nail art, or paraffin wax treatments, to enhance the experience. This treatment not only improves the appearance of your nails but also leaves your hands feeling soft, smooth, and refreshed.
